"Bounce Ritual" is movement encoded.

Built on a swung, body-first groove, the track channels bounce as a form of control — elastic, hypnotic, and deliberate. The bassline drives forward with a loose, kinetic swagger while percussive accents and breakbeat inflections destabilize expectation just enough to keep the system alive.

Beneath it all, distant choral tones emerge — not as a focal point, but as a signal. A reminder that repetition becomes ritual, and rhythm becomes instruction.
