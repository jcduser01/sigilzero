"Drums in the Deep" operates on pressure and patterns — a low, pulsating bassline anchored beneath heavy, deliberate drums.

The arrangement is sparse by design. Each element is given space to land with weight, while eerie, sci-fi atmospherics move at the edges of the mix. The result is controlled tension — a track that feels restrained, but never static.

Built for late-night rooms where attitude matters more than excess. A functional driver for DJs working in darker tech house territory.
