"Afterphase" captures the system state after peak intensity — when motion persists, but begins to resolve.

Built on heavy, driving drums and a restrained, synthetic lead, the track moves with precision rather than force. Energy is sustained, not escalated — held in a controlled, late-night equilibrium designed for DJs navigating deeper into a set. A functional tool for floors that favor discipline over spectacle.
